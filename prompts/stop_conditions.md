# Stop Conditions

Stop investigating when one or more of these are true:

- Confidence is high enough for a credible triage conclusion.
- The next available probes are low-value or repetitive.
- The remaining steps cross a policy boundary.
- The issue clearly requires L3 / platform engineering intervention.
- The probe budget has been used.

When stopping, write:
- current assessment
- evidence used
- confidence level
- recommended next action
- escalation target if needed
